Our Work

The Salon

Salon Ricochet offers exemplary hair services in an outstanding new location on the corner of Juniper and Sansom Streets in Center City Philadelphia. The team of highly talented stylist specialize in cuts, color, styling, keratin straightening and hair extensions. The salon, housed in the historic Philadelphia Building, has been a fixture of Mid-town Village since owner Kurt Darhower opened its doors in 2005. The mission for Salon Ricochet has always been to combine highly skilled services with a friendly, fun and relaxed environment. This vision has helped the salon to attract an eclectic artistic team and outstanding loyal clientele.
